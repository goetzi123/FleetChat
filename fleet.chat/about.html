Goetz Weber is the founder and CEO of Fleet.Chat. Goetz brings years of experience in enterprise software and a commitment to solving real-world problems that impact thousands of drivers and fleet operators daily.

Our Mission

Fleet.Chat exists to bridge the communication gap between fleet management systems and drivers. We believe that seamless, real-time communication is essential for efficient fleet operations. Our bidirectional communication protocol connects Samsara, Motive, and other fleet management platforms directly to WhatsApp, enabling instant messaging without requiring drivers to learn new apps or systems.
