About Fleet.Chat

Fleet.Chat is a bidirectional communication protocol service that integrates with multiple fleet management systems including Samsara, Motive, and Geotab to facilitate WhatsApp messaging with drivers. Founded by Goetz Weber, the company operates under strict boundaries as communication middleware only, maintaining driver phone number mappings and encrypted API credentials while processing templated WhatsApp messages triggered by fleet system events.
